# Content Editing Framework (For Later Use)

## Purpose
Maintain tone while editing or expanding content.

## Sentence Rules
- Short, declarative lines
- One idea per sentence
- Silence is acceptable

## Adjective Discipline
Allowed only if they add meaning.
Avoid:
best, leading, premium, world-class, innovative

## Tone
- Judgment over hype
- Calm over clever
- Confidence without claims

## Rewrite Test
After writing, ask:
Can this be simpler?
Can this be quieter?
Can this be removed?
